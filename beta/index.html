Alright yinz, here's the deal.

Type yer bid, pick yer warehouse — I'll show ya what it really costs out the door. Fees, tax, all of it.

Two tabs, two questions. "What will I pay?" runs the math forward. "What should I bid?" — give me yer budget and I'll tell ya the most you can bid and stay under it.

Them little pins? Tap one gold and that number rides along when ya flip tabs. Gray means it stays put on its own tab.

More Options holds the assurance fee, transfer fee, and Mac Bucks. Assurance ain't taxed — I checked the receipts.

Bid war heatin' up? Smash that + button. A buck a tap.

Flippin' it? Profit Check tab: tell me what it resells for and what ya want outta the deal — I'll tell ya the most you can bid and still get paid.

Share sends yer exact numbers to a buddy. And hit "Add to Home Screen" — I work offline in the warehouse n'at.

Pin a number

A gold pin keeps that number on both tabs when you flip back and forth.

Gray pin = the number belongs to this tab only.

Tap any pin to switch it.
